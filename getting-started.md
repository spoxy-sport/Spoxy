# Getting Started with SPOXY

## 01 · Connect Your Wallet

Connect a Solana wallet (like Phantom) to SPOXY, then deposit funds to start creating or joining prediction challenges. No sign-up forms, no KYC gates.

## 02 · Creating Challenges

Pick a market — crypto price action, sports outcomes, or anything else — define the condition, timeframe, and stake the collateral that funds the reward pool.

## 03 · Joining Battles

Browse open challenges on the board, pick the side you believe in, and lock in your stake. Every challenge is PvP: your win is another player's loss.

## 04 · Resolution

Crypto markets settle automatically via price oracles. Sports and off-chain markets are verified by SPOXY Masters NFT holders through transparent on-chain voting.

## 05 · Payouts

Once a challenge resolves, the reward pool is distributed automatically to winning participants' wallets — no manual claims, no delays.

## 06 · Governance

SPOXY Masters NFT holders help validate disputed outcomes and shape platform parameters, keeping resolution fair and community-driven.

## FAQ

**What is SPOXY?**
SPOXY is the PvP battleground for predictions where users can create, join, and compete in challenges to prove their skills and win rewards.

**How do challenges work?**
Challenges are set up with specific conditions and timeframes. Users can enter these challenges, and the winners are determined based on the outcome of the event.

**Who can create a challenge on SPOXY?**
Any user can create a challenge, provided they set the necessary parameters and provide the required collateral for the reward pool.

**How are challenges resolved?**
Through a combination of automated price oracles for crypto and community validation. For sports events, SPOXY Masters NFT holders help validate the outcome to ensure fairness.

**How can I deposit and withdraw funds?**
You can deposit funds directly from your Solana wallet into the platform. Withdrawals can be made at any time.
